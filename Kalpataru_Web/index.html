Our Heritage

For Those Who Know Real Spice.

Since 1980 at Kalpataru Atta Mill, we believe that the soul of Indian cooking lies in the purity of its spices. Located in Siliguri, we bypass middlemen and commercial shortcuts to bring you spices exactly as nature intended.

We use traditional slow-grinding (Chakki) methods that ensure the natural essential oils and aromas of the spices are locked in, never burnt away by high-speed commercial machines.

  • Hand-picked raw ingredients from trusted farmers.
  • Slow cold-grinding technology.
  • No added starch, brick powder, or artificial dyes.

Why Kalpataru?

What sets our milled spices apart from supermarket brands.

Zero Adulteration

We guarantee absolutely no sawdust, brick powder, or artificial colorants in our spices. 100% purity you can trust.

Slow Cold-Grinding

High heat destroys spice flavor. Our traditional slow chakkis keep temperatures low, preserving essential natural oils.

Aroma-Lock Packing

Packed immediately after grinding in food-grade materials to ensure the aroma stays intact until it reaches your kitchen.

Hand-Sorted Raw Material

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

Are there any added colors in your spices?
Absolutely not. The vibrant colors of our Turmeric and Red Chilli come naturally from sourcing high-quality raw ingredients. We strictly prohibit artificial dyes.
What is the shelf life of your milled spices?
For maximum freshness and aroma, we recommend using our spices within 6 to 9 months of purchase. Store them in an airtight container away from direct sunlight.
Do you deliver outside Siliguri?
Currently, we focus on local deliveries in and around Siliguri to ensure freshness, but we are expanding our logistics. Please message us on WhatsApp with your pin code to confirm!
Do you offer bulk pricing for restaurants/caterers?
Yes! We provide special pricing for bulk orders. Please call us directly to discuss wholesale requirements.
